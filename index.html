Notre Histoire

The Art of French Living

Café Lilou was born from a passion for Parisian café culture — the clink of porcelain, the aroma of fresh croissants, and conversations that linger for hours. Located in the elegant Seef District, our restaurant blends timeless French recipes with the warmth of Bahraini hospitality.

Our pastry chefs arrive before dawn to prepare buttery viennoiseries, while our kitchen brigade crafts classics like coq au vin and bouillabaisse with a modern touch.
